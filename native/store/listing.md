## Full description (max 4000)
Cardinal OS is one calm, premium home for your whole life. Habits, tasks,
projects, goals, calendar, money, body and mind — together in one place, with AI
woven through everything and one honest number to orient by.

WHY CARDINAL
• One number, every morning. Your Life Score folds your whole life into a single
  figure from 0 to 1000 — the first thing you check, the thing you orient around.
  It rewards balance, not grinding, and never punishes a single off day.
• Not a template. A system. Unlike a document you maintain, Cardinal runs itself —
  noticing the patterns that only appear when everything shares one home.
• Calm by design. No streak-shaming, no anxiety, no red. Premium and quiet.

WHAT'S INSIDE
• Habits — a gentle contribution grid and streaks that forgive.
• Tasks — natural language capture: “Call Anuj tomorrow 5pm” becomes a task.
• Projects — turn a project into next actions with one tap.
• Calendar — two-way Google Calendar sync; schedule in plain language.
• Journal & Notes — your second brain; ask your own notes anything.
• Goals, Money, Health and more — with life packs like Exam Prep.

AI THROUGHOUT
A daily briefing that knows what matters, weekly reviews, and insights like
“your spending spikes when your sleep drops.”

PRIVATE BY DEFAULT
Your data is yours. Export everything any time. Optional app lock with your
fingerprint, face, or device passcode.

Free during beta. No card, no catch.
